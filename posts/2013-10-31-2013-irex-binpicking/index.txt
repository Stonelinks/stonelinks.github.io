9:T4bb,<p>Back in November I had the chance to take a survey of all the different binpicking systems at the International Robotics Exhibition (IREX) in Tokyo, Japan. <a href="http://www.nikkan.co.jp/eve/irex/english/">IREX</a> is massive, the world's largest robotics expo. It happens every two years and is chock full of impressive demos. This year my favorite had to be the <a href="http://youtu.be/QR4lG98Ehng">full scale welding line</a> that Mitsubishi made. I was there with MUJIN and even had my picture on a few advertisements:</p>
<p><img src="/2013-10-31-2013-irex-binpicking/IMG_20131107_102636.jpg" alt=""></p>
<p>As a small robot company just starting out at trying to make one of these binpicking systems, I felt it was important to make a survey of these things. Maybe someone other than us will find this exhaustive analysis useful. I've included videos of each (got some really nasty looks trying to record them)!</p>
